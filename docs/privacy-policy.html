Privacy Policy for TempoFlow

Last Updated: December 23, 2025

Introduction

TempoFlow is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how TempoFlow handles information when you use our mobile and web application.

Information Collection and Use

TempoFlow does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

What We DON'T Collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Device information
  • Crash reports or diagnostics
  • Any form of user tracking data

Data Storage

All data created within TempoFlow is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. This includes:

  • Tempo flow configurations
  • Saved presets
  • Sound preferences
  • App settings

This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any servers
  • Is not accessible to us or any third parties
  • Can be deleted at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app

Internet Connection

TempoFlow is designed to work completely offline. No internet connection is required to use any features of the app. The app does not make any network requests.

Third-Party Services

TempoFlow does not integrate with or use any third-party services, including:

  • Analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics)
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media platforms
  • Cloud storage services
  • Crash reporting services

Permissions

The app may request the following device permissions:

  • Audio: Required to play metronome sounds

These permissions are used solely for app functionality and do not result in any data collection.

Children's Privacy

TempoFlow does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • You can delete all app data by clearing browser data/cache
  • On Android, you can clear app data in Settings → Apps → TempoFlow → Storage → Clear Data
  • Uninstalling the app will permanently remove all associated data

Open Source

TempoFlow is an open-source project. You can review the source code to verify our privacy practices at: GitHub Repository

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Contact Us

  • Email: ezequielcompo@gmail.com

Summary

In short: TempoFlow is a privacy-first application that operates entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or share any of your data. Your information never leaves your device.
